Project Name

Extremis is a third-person 3D shooter that relies on stealth and role performance to survive and win the game. Each player is assigned a faction and has to perform its role, always with surviving in mind, each shot being heard by players and a monster that lurks the map, searching for survivors to kill, if you die, the game ends for you. Perform your role well and you will earn faction points, lose your sanity and you will become a villain. Each match is a unique experience for the player thanks to a custom balancing system. Loot and kill in a totally PvP arena.
